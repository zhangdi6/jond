# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Recruiters, collaborators, founders, and product-minded peers who want to quickly understand Zhang Di / Jond's craft, shipping history, and fit for mobile or social-product work.

## Product Purpose

The site presents Jond as a product-oriented developer who can take mobile and social app ideas from concept through release. Success means a visitor understands the work, trusts the execution quality, and has a clear next action: view shipped projects, watch the intro, or open the resume.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, precise, and social-native. The voice should feel young and high-agency without becoming gimmicky or noisy.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic AI portfolio templates, repeated equal-card grids, beige craft palettes, tiny uppercase labels on every section, and decorative effects that do not support the work.

## Design Principles

- Show shipped products before abstract claims.
- Let the shader background feel like a product signal, not a decorative wallpaper.
- Use asymmetry and strong type to create a memorable first impression.
- Keep contact, resume, and store links immediately discoverable.
- Prefer clear hierarchy and fast scanning over dense self-description.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, visible keyboard focus, responsive layouts without horizontal overflow, and reduced-motion fallbacks for animation-heavy surfaces.
